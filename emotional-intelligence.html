Emotional Intelligence

    1. What is EQ?

  • What is EQ? EQ is emotional intelligence, it’s a set of skills that contribute towards effective relationships between people. Also it contributes towards the wellbeing of yourself. It includes recognizing and understanding your own personal emotions. Also using your emotions to achieve personal goals.

    • 2. How is EQ different to IQ?

  • IQ measures how smart you are in the terms of normal subjects that you could do at school. Science, math and stuff like that. It’s also like a score of how good you’re doing, the higher the better.

    • 3. How is EQ important?

  • EQ is important for many reasons, some reasons are: It enhances communication skills by improving the understandings of emotions, it can help you build stronger relationships, it helps you navigate through conflict and it helps with leadership. These are all great things to have.
